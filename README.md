ok it's not great, and it's not how i would want it to be finished, but here's a quick guide on how to play:


<i>PLEASE READ BEFORE PLAYING :)

hello young rumour!

you might be wondering: why are you here? who brought you into existence? what even is a rumour? (for that one you're gonna need a dictionary)

the answer to your questions lies here - in sleepy town. there might only be one house - but it's like there's 10! that's right - a sleepover is happening.

you'd be surprised at how many of you lot - rumours, that is - can be created in a single night!

anyways, getting around should be pretty easy: you can use the WASD or arrow keys, and if you wanna pop in to the sleepover house, just stick around on their doorstep for long enough and i'm sure they'll let you in.

oh and one more thing - once you think you know who the last person to find out about you was, and who told them, and who told that person, and the person before that, etc. etc... just press "E" to save your trail!</i>
